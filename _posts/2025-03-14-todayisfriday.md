---
title: "Reset Intel MacBook SMC"
categories: mac
---

Intel MacBook Pro with built-in battery (2018 and earlier, plus some later Intel models)

1. Shut down the Mac completely
2. On the built-in keyboard, press and hold:
Left Control (⌃)
Left Option (⌥)
Right Shift (⇧)
3. Keep holding those for 7 seconds
4. Without releasing, also press and hold the Power button
5. Hold all four keys for another 7 seconds
6. Release all keys
7. Wait 5–10 seconds
8. Press Power to turn the Mac back on

✔️ SMC is now reset.
